We also have two collections of collections:
  • #$ExistingObjectType Instances include collections of tangible things.

  • #$OrganismClassificationType Instances are collections of living things that divide them up taxonomically, such as #$Vertebrate, #$Invertebrate, #$Mammal, #$Dog, etc.
