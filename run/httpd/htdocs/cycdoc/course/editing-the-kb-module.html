Editing operations modify the KB of your OpenCyc Server (the one which you selected in the "Select OpenCyc Server" page). Your server usually keeps a transcript of its operations and it may share operations with other OpenCyc Servers by means of common transcripts.

Before you modify the KB - especially if you are only experimenting with OpenCyc - you should know how your operations will be recorded and with whom (if anyone) they will be shared.

The first thing you must do is to identify yourself to the system, so we'll start with the Login facility.
