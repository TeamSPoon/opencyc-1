Notes on finding good denotations
If you believe that the word should already be in the lexicon (and you just want to add strings to the entry), but CYC® claims that no word unit exists, then:

  • Check to be sure that the word you have entered is indeed a root word.
  • Try entering variations of the word to see if such an entry exists.
  • If the word still fails to turn up, proceed with creating a new word constant.
