Creating a New Constant
Renaming a Constant
Sometimes, it is convenient to create a constant with the same assertions as another. Perhaps you would like to create another #$Cyclist constant for someone at your site with the same assertions you have on your constant. Click on your constant and then click on [Create Similar]. You will be prompted for the name of the new constant. Enter the new constant name and then select the assertions you would like to have connected to the new constant. When you click the "Create Similar" button, the assertions will be added to the queue. If the new constant you entered already is a Cyc constant then you will receive an error.
